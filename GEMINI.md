## What To Remember

Shrimo Verse is a cinematic interactive universe, not a normal website.

Use:

- black/cyan visual identity,
- scene-first flow,
- mobile-first layouts,
- one focus at a time,
- clean HUD controls,
- no patch stacking.

Do not create website-like cards, dashboards, heavy borders, article panels, or random override files.

---

## Before Coding

Prepare:

```txt
1. Audit
2. Root cause
3. Files affected
4. Scene impact
5. Mobile/tablet/desktop effect
6. Animation triggers
7. Validation plan
```
