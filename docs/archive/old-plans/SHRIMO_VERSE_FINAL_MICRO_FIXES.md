## How users see past projects

Users can view past/project work by moving to the **Product Gallery** step:

- click the right arrow until Product Gallery opens,
- click the Product Gallery bullet,
- use Auto Flight until it reaches Product Gallery,
- or use Free Explore / guide controls.

Inside Product Gallery, product dots open each focused project panel. Current project paths include Shrimo Innovations, Digiting Card, Photocopywala, and Business Directory Platform.

## Guided Flight vs Replay Guide

- **Guided Flight** starts the guided learning flow for the Shrimo Verse controls and experience.
- **Replay Guide** restarts that same guide again after the visitor has already entered or closed it.
